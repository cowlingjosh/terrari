# `terrari`

</div>

An operating system of sorts, but more importantly, an apology.

> Warning: This is a way for me to be creative and journal over the next few months at least while I am adjusting to a new life, so the style will be quite idiosyncratic.

## Etymology

As a hint of things to come, the name originally comes from a mixture of _terra_ and _mosaic_.
_terraic_ didn't sound right so I added an extra 'r', partly just to make the child inside of me who still loves dinosaurs happy.
I then wanted a sick domain name, found `terrari.co` then a few days later I realised I liked `terrari` more...
Naming is hard!
